MAKING TREATS!

NAVIGATING LINKS
Bannana treats: A little less than 1/2 cup of pellets. 1/4 cup oats. 1 bannana. 1/2 of a big carrot, or 1 small carrot. Steps:Crush up pellets. Grind up oats into powder. Mix the pellets with the oats. Mash up bannana. Cut/grind the carrot. Add the carrots with the bannanas. Mix all of the ingredients together. Let the mixture sit for 10 minutes. Add a little bit of water. Heat the oven to 350*, bake with foil sheet on tray. Bake for 30 minutes. Then freeze in the freezer. Then, feed them to your rabbit!
